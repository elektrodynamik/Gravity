## Wiederholung:

$$ -\Delta \phi = 0 \text{ (L)} $$

$$ -\Delta \phi = 4 \pi \rho \text{ (P) } $$

$$ \text{D-RB}: \phi(\vec x) = \int_V {\rm d}^3 x' G(\vec x,\vec x') \rho(\vec x') - \dfrac{1}{4\pi} \int \phi(\vec x') \partial_{\vec n'} G(\vec x,\vec x') $$

$$ \text{N-RB}: \int {\rm d}^3 x' G(\vec x,\vec x') \rho(\vec x') + \dfrac{1}{4 \pi} G(\vec x,\vec x') \partial_{\vec n'} \phi(\vec x') + \langle \phi \rangle $$
